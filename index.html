Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables,meats.

    Prepration Time:

  • Total: Approximately 10 mins
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter and oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

    Instructions

  1. 1. Beat the Eggs: In a bowl beat the egss with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk to have fluffier texture.
  2. 2. Heat The Pan: Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. 3. Cook the omellete: Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  4. 4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to settle at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of omlette.
  5. 5. Fold and Serve: As the omellete cooks fold it over the fillings.Let it cook another minute,then slide it onto plate
  6. 6. Enjoy: Serve hot with additional salt and pepper if needed.
